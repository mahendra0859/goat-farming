What is Goat Contract Farming?

  • Goat Contract farming (GCF) can defined as Goat production carried out according to an agreement between a buyer and farmers, which establishes conditions for the production and marketing of a Goat products.

  • In turn, the buyer commits to purchase the Goat.

  • Advances given by the sponsor to the producer can be recover from the sale proceeds of the produce.

  • Typically, the farmer agrees to provide agreed quantities of a Goat product.

  • These should meet the quality standards of the purchaser and be supplied at the time determined by the purchaser.

What it means for purchaser?

  • Protection from fluctuation in market pricing.

  • Long term planning made possible

  • Dedicated supplier base

Term & Condition of Goat Contract farming (GCF)

  • Flexible Rearing instalments (1month/ 3months/ 6months/1Year)

  • Guaranteed Weight Gain

  • Money back if Guaranteed Weight Gain is not achieved

  • Goat replacement in case of Death

  • Every month customer gets the update on Goat ( photos/ current weight and health condition)

  • Free home delivery along with 2days of feed
